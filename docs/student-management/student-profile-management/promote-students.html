Promote students

Now that your students have finished a year of TypingClub, it is time to move them and their progress along to the next grade. This is easily accomplished by either using the Move Up 1 Grade button or uploading an updated .csv file.


Method One

To simply move your students up one grade, but not change any other profile details or their enrollments, use this method:

  1. Go to the Students tab.
  2. Click the checkboxes next students who need to be moved up a grade.
  3. Click the Move Up 1 Grade button.

Method Two

  1. Prepare: To update your student profiles, including grade and other details such as username, email, etc., you can export a .csv file of students currently in your account by going to the Students tab on the left side of the page. You will see the list of students in the account, as well as a few filters on the top of the student list. Filter for the grade you wish to change, and click the Export CSV button to download the .csv file for the desired grade.
  2. Save: Open the spreadsheet and update your students' information. You can also add new students to the list to upload them to your account. Save your file and then click the Update Many Students tab. You will see information about what you can include in your .csv file. Click Next Step for more info, and then click Next Step again.
  3. Upload: Click Choose File to select your .csv file and click Next Step again to upload the file.
  4. Preview Data: You will see a preview of your list of students and headings for each column. You can change these headings if they are incorrect. If all these students belong to the same class, you can enroll them in a class using the drop down list at the bottom of this page. Otherwise, simply click Finalize Import.
  5. Finalize:

    Each record will either have a green Success or red Failed label as well as a note regarding the status. Review your results to ensure that each student was updated successfully.

You will see that any new info you included in the .csv file is now reflected in the system under each student!
