Enroll students

Each student must be enrolled in a class in order to have lessons to work on. Depending on the method you choose to create your student accounts, this can be a one step process (Google Classroom, class codes, Add Many Students Wizard one roster at a time) or it can be a two-step process in which you add classes and then enroll students.


Enroll students using Google Classroom

Rostering from Google Classroom will both create your student accounts and roster your classes in one step. If your students have already been added to your TypingClub account using their Google email addresses, it will enroll them in their classes.

  1. Go to the Classes tab.
  2. Click Google Classroom.
  3. Click Get Started.
  4. Log in to your Google account.
  5. Click the Import button for each class you'd like to import.
  6. Your classes will appear in the Classes tab.



Allow students to enroll with class codes

You can allow students enroll in your class using a class code. For more details, see our Class Codes help section.

  1. Go to the Classes tab and choose the class you want students to be able to join.
  2. On the right side of the screen, check the box next to Students can join this class.
  3. Share the class code or the URL with students.

Enroll students using the Update Many Students Wizard

Create a csv spreadsheet for each of your classes and import them using the wizard. Before finalizing the import, you can choose a class to enroll the students in.

  1. Add your classes.
  2. Create a csv file for each class.
  3. Go to the Students tab and click the Update Many Students tab.
  4. Review the instructions if needed and then click Next step twice.
  5. Upload your file.
  6. Preview your data. You can change the column headings if needed.
  7. Choose the class from the drop-down menu before finalizing your import.
  8. Review your results.

Enroll from the Students tab

You can select multiple students by using the checkboxes and then click Enroll in Class at the top of the student list. Use this method if your students are already in your account and you only have a couple of classes to roster.

  1. Go to the Students tab.
  2. Check the box next to each of the students that will be enrolled.
  3. Click the Enroll in a Class button.
  4. Click Enroll next to the appropriate class.
