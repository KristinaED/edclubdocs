Add students

If you have a small number of students, you can choose to add them to the program individually.

  1. Go to the Students tab.
  2. Click the Add Student tab at the top of the screen.
  3. Fill out the form with the new student's details.
  4. Click Add Student at the bottom of the screen.



When you are finished, you will see the new student appear in the list on your Students tab.
