Tracking students' typing accuracy

If you have a subscription to TypingClub School Edition, you will have access to advanced reporting tools to monitor your students' progress in terms of accuracy. Accuracy is calculated in two ways:

  • Accuracy: Accuracy shows the final accuracy while taking into account and not penalizing students for corrections made during typing practice. This value is generally higher than the real accuracy.

  • Real Accuracy: This is the raw, uncorrected accuracy. Even if the use of backspace is allowed, this value shows the percentage of text that was typed without mistakes in the first place.

School-Based Accuracy Reports

To assess the typing accuracy of your school, go to the Reports tab and open the Schools tab. There you can use the filters to view aggregate school data for a selected time frame, show the growth on detailed view, and export the report into a spreadsheet.



Class-Based Accuracy Reports

This report allows you to track students' accuracy. It can be found by going to the Classes tab, opening your class, going to the Reports tab, and clicking Activity Summary Report. This report includes a range of data including the average accuracy per student. Teachers can run the report for any given time period. The activity sheet is exportable into a CSV file.



For more detailed information, go to the Reports tab and open the Class tab. There you can use the filters to view class data for a selected time frame, show the growth on detailed view, and export the report into a spreadsheet.



Lesson Plan-Based Accuracy Reports

This chart allows you to monitor the evolution of typing accuracy in your class. The report can be found by going to the Classes tab, opening your class, clicking the lesson plan tab of choice, clicking Stats, and scrolling down to the chart titled Progress Overview. Click the Accuracy % box to view the evolution of typing accuracy on a given lesson plan in your class.



Student-Based Accuracy Reports

This set of reports allows you to view the typing speed for an individual student. To view these reports, go to the Students tab, select your student, and once looking at the student's profile overview, scroll down to review the following charts:

  • Progress Overview: Click the Accuracy % box to view the individual student's evolution of typing accuracy on a given lesson plan.

  • Finger Mastery: The chart shows the accuracy for each individual hand and finger.

  • Speed and Accuracy per Character: The chart displays student's most accurate and least accurate character.

  • Print Friendly Report: Teachers can generate a PDF document which summarizes student's daily activity including their accuracy.
