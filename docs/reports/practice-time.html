Tracking students' practice time

If you have a subscription to TypingClub School Edition, you will have access to advanced reporting tools that break down your students' time spent practicing. This is especially useful if you prefer to grade students based on how much time they spend practicing their typing.

School-Based Time Reports

To view the practice time for a school, go to the Reports tab and open the Schools tab. There you can use the filters to view aggregate school data for a selected time frame, show the growth on detailed view, and export the report into a spreadsheet.



Class-Based Time Reports

This set of reports allows you to track students' practice time in a class. The total time isn't broken down to individual lesson plans. Helpful time-based reports can be found by going to the Classes tab, opening your class, and checking one of the reports available under the Reports tab.



  • Daily Time Report: time sheet exportable into a CSV file
  • Weekly Time Report: time sheet exportable into a CSV file
  • Activity Summary Report: activity sheet exportable into a CSV file

For more detailed information, go to the Reports tab and open the Class tab. There you can use the filters to view class data for a selected time frame, show the growth on detailed view, and export the report into a spreadsheet.



Additional charts to review practice time in a class are available by going to the Classes tab, opening your class, and clicking Overview:

  • Active Practice Time: This chart shows a total practice time (only time spent typing) for the class, and breaks the time into passed, failed, and partial attempts. It compares your class performance to other classes in the same grade level and account. This chart can't be exported at this time.



  • Typing Activity: Click the Practice Time box to view the history of practice time in your class. This chart can't be exported at this time.



  • Punchcard: The Punchcard displays the times when most practice happens - the larger the circle, the more time spent typing. This chart can't be exported at this time.



  • Practice Calendar: The calendar shows the daily practice time for the past 12 months. This chart can't be exported at this time.



Notice: The above reports are NOT lesson plan specific. They include total time spent typing in a given class, regardless of the lesson plan.

Lesson Plan-Based Time Reports

This set of reports allows you to track class's practice time on a specific lesson plan. These reports can be found by going to the Classes tab, opening your class, clicking the lesson plan tab of choice, and clicking Stats. They closely resemble the class-wide graphs shown above, except these include only time spent on a given lesson plan and not the whole class.

  • Active Practice Time: This chart shows a total practice time (only time spent typing) for the lesson plan in question, and breaks the time into passed, failed, and partial attempts. It compares your class performance on the lesson plan to other classes in the same grade level and account.

  • Typing Activity: Click the "Practice Time" box to view the history of practice time on the given lesson plan in your class.

  • Punchcard:The Punchcard displays the times when most practice takes place - the larger the circle, the more time spent typing.

  • Practice Calendar:The calendar shows the daily practice time on the given lesson plan for the past 12 months.
Notice: The above reports are lesson plan specific. They include total time spent typing on a specific lesson plan.

Student-Based Time Reports

Teachers can view students' practice time either individually or in bulk for an entire class or school:

  • To view the practice time for an individual student, go to the Students tab, find your student, and click Manage Progress. The student's progress is displayed separately for each lesson plan. You can click the individual lesson plans that the student has worked on to see his/her overall time spent on each of them.

  • To view the practice time for an entire class or school, go to the Reports tab, open the Students tab, and use the filters to narrown the report down by class, grade, school, or time.
