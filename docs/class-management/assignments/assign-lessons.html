Assigning a specific group of lessons

You can limit which lessons your students have access to on a given lesson plan by assigning a group of lessons. This will help keep the class together and ensure that they are focusing on a given skill or area.


  1. Click the Classes tab on the left side of the screen. Choose the class of interest and select Lesson Plans. In line with each lesson plan you will see the option to Limit Progress.


  2. Click the link that says Not Limited. Select the second option and enter the lesson numbers you'd like to assign to your class.


  3. Click Update. The words "Not Limited" have been replaced with the range of lessons you selected.


  4. Assigned lessons will appear on the student interface with a solid blue strip on the bottom of the lesson icon. If students attempt to practice lessons that have't been assigned, they will be presented with a written notice advising them to work on assigned lessons instead.
