Assigning multiple instructors to classes

Every class on TypingClub can be managed by multiple instructors. The teacher who creates a class in the first place is automatically assigned to it. To add more instructors, follow the next steps:

  1. Go to the Classes tab.
  2. Open your class.
  3. Click the Instructors link.
  4. Click Add instructor to this class.
Note: Make sure that the instructor already exists in your account. If the teacher doesn't appear in the list of instructors, you'll have to reach out to the account admin who can add the instructor to your account.
