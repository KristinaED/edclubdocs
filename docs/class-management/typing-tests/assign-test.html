Assigning tests to students

Once you've created a test, you need to assign it to your students. This is a two step process explained below:

  1. Select who can take the test

    You can do this by selecting the Typing Tests tab on the left side of the page, and clicking the test you want to assign. From the Edit Options tab, you can select who can take the test:

    • Only the students I select: You will need to manually choose students to take the test.
    • Any student in my school: Students from your school will need to sign up for the test from their student portal. When they log in, they will see a button labeled Add more activities (1). They must click this button to access the test.
    • Any student in my account: Students will need to sign up for the test from their student portal. When they log in, they will see a button labeled Add more activities (1). They must click this button to access the test.

  2. Assign the test to select students and classes

    If you chose the first option, the next step is to select students who can take the test. To do this, click on Students and search for each student individually, or assign the test to an entire class.
