Managing the jump-ahead behavior

The default settings don't allow students to jump ahead in the lesson plan. We believe that students should practice lesson in order to get the most out of the program. If you prefer that your students have the freedom to jump around the lesson plan, please follow these steps:

  1. Click the Classes tab on the left side of the screen.
  2. Click on the class for which you would like to change this setting.
  3. Under the Manage Class tab, click on the Edit Class sub-tab. This will be the 5th link from the left.
  4. Scroll down until you see Allow Jumping Ahead. This is near the bottom of the page.
  5. Select your preferred behavior.
  6. To save your changes, press Update Settings at the bottom of the screen.
