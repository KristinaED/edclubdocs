Logging in to TypingClub with Google

Teachers and students can log in to your TypingClub School account using their Google accounts. To allow your teachers and students to log in with Google, include their Google email address in the Email field when creating their profiles. When you roster students from Google Classroom, this is done automatically. Students do not need to have access to email in order to use Google Single Sign On.

Tip:You do not need to add a username or password if students will log in with single sign on.

By default, your TypingClub portal allows users to log in with Google or a username and password. To edit these options, follow the steps below:

  1. Sign in to your teacher portal
  2. Go to the Settings tab
  3. Click Account Options
  4. Navigate to the Login Options section
  5. Choose the options you would like to have available on your portal
  6. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click Update to save your changes.
