How do I grade my typing class?


If you are unsure of how to grade your students, we suggest three approaches that have worked well for teachers in the past.

Grading Suggestion 1: Time

Grade students based on the amount of time they spend practicing typing on a daily or weekly basis. Helpful time-based reports can be found under Classes > Your class > Reports.

Grading Suggestion 2: Stars

Grade students based on the number of stars they earn on each lesson. The more stars students receive, the better their standing on the scoreboard. The scoreboard is unique to each lesson plan, you can find it under Classes > Your class > Your lesson plan > Scoreboard.

Grading suggestion 3: Test

Grade students based on their scores on Typing Tests. The Test Results page shows the best attempt for each student. The list can be reorganized by clicking any of the headers.
