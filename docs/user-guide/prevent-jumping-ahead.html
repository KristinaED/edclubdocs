How do I prevent students from jumping ahead?

If you would like students to complete their lessons in order, please follow these steps:

  1. Click the Classes tab on the left side of the screen.
  2. Click on the class for which you would like to change this setting.
  3. Under the Manage Class tab, click on the Edit Class sub-tab. This will be the 5th tab from the left.
  4. Scroll down until you see Allow Jumping Ahead. This is near the bottom of the page.
  5. Select Students are not allowed to jump ahead.
  6. To save your changes, press Update Settings at the bottom of the screen.
