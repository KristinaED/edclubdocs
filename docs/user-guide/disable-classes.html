How do I disable classes?

Why disable classes?

When you disable a class, students will not be able to access the lesson plans that are assigned to that class. You can use this feature to either hide certain lesson plans from students or to preserve a roster for a later date.

Where do they go?

Disabled classes are hidden from your class list. To view your disabled classes, click the Show Filters option and then select the box for Inactive or Both active and inactive.

How to Disable Classes

  1. Go to the Classes tab
  2. Check the box for the class(es) you'd like to disable
  3. Click the Disable button at the top of the list of classes

How to Activate Classes

  1. Go to the Classes tab
  2. Click the Show Filters button on the right side of the screen
  3. Under Classes that are, select either Inactive or Both active and inactive
  4. Check the box next to the class(es) you'd like to activate
  5. Click the Activate button at the top of the list of classes

How to View Disabled Classes

  1. Go to the Classes tab
  2. Click the Show Filters button on the right side of the screen
  3. Under Classes that are, select either Inactive or Both active and inactive
