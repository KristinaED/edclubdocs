Teacher & Admin Password Reset

Teachers set up their password on their own, and their passwords are not available to view by account administrators. If you are a teacher and you forgot your password, you can choose one of the next options to retrieve it:


  • Account administrators can change instructor passwords:

    1. Go to the Instructors tab.
    2. Click open the instructor's profile.
    3. Click Change Password.

  • Admins and instructors can change their own passwords:

    1. Go to the Settings tab.
    2. Click Change Password.

  • Admins and instructors can reset a forgotten password:

    1. Go to your school's unique login URL.
    2. Click Forgot Password?.
Tip: Instructor passwords must be 8 characaters or more.
