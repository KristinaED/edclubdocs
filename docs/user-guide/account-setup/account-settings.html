Settings

As an Account Administrator, the Settings tab gives you acccess to set up your account-wide settings.

Personal Settings

  • Change Password
    • Change your personal password.
  • Update Personal Information
    • Update your personal contact info.

Account Settings

  • Update Account Information
    • Update the account name and address.
  • Schools
    • View the list of schools in your account.
  • Account Options
    • Google Login Behavior
      • Decide if students are logged out of both TypingClub and Google when logging out.
    • Logout URL
      • Choose the URL your students land on when logging out.
    • Automatic User Sign Out
      • Decide if users should be automatically signed out after a given period of inactivity.
    • Login Options
      • Choose how your students may sign into your account:
        • Username/password
        • Google
        • Facebook
        • Microsoft 365
    • Allow Scoreboard
      • Use this option if you wish to disable the scoreboard account-wide.
    • Is your account searchable?
      • If this option is enabled, users will be able to find your account if they search for their email address, school name or account's name.
  • Activity Logs
    • View the history of significant activity in your account such as student and teacher profiles being created/deleted/updated.
  • Instructor Options
    • Student Visibility: either allow teachers to only view students that are enrolled in their classes or all students in their school.
    • Manage students: decide if teachers can add/edit students.
    • Custom Lesson Plans: decide if teachers can create their own lesson plans.
    • Creating Tests: decide if students can create tests.
  • Student Options
    • Password Reset: decide if students can change their own passwords.
    • Track Partial Attempts: decide if your account tracks partial attempts or only completed attempts.
  • Time Zone
    • Set the time zone for your account.

Account Data

  • Data Import Tool
    • This tool will allow you to create schools, classes, instructors, and students using CSV files. For more info, check out the Data Import Tool Process PDF.
  • Clever
    • Clever synced accounts can sync monitor status and adjust settings here. For more, take a look at the next article, Clever settings.
  • Manage Account Data
    • Use this tool to delete either class, student, or instructor data per quarter.
  • Delete Account
    • Should you need to delete your account, you can do so here.
