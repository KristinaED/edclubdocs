Creating student accounts individually

Students can be added to a TypingClub account either individually or in bulk via a CSV file, Google Classroom, Clever, or ClassLink. If you have a small number of students, you can choose to add them to the program individually.

  1. Go to your Students tab on the left side of your screen.
  2. Click Add Student on the top.
  3. Fill out a form with information about the new student.

When you are finished, you will see the student appear in your Students tab. To learn how to upload students in bulk, please review the article titled Adding Many Students.
